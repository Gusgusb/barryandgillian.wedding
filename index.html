We recommend one of two options.

Option 1: There is limited accommodation available for our guests in two self catering cottages. The guest houses are located just outside the main castle entrance approximately stumbling distance from Darver Castle.

To enquire about the Guest Cottages, please contact Darver Castle directly;

  • Phone: +353 (0)42 9379466
  • Email: oakwoodcottages@darvercastle.ie

Option 2: Due to the limited accomodation around the Castle, we expect the majority of guests to stay in The Crown Plaza Hotel in Dundalk, 4 miles away from Darver Castle.

The Crown Plaza provides a shuttle bus to and from Darver Castle on the day of the wedding so guests can leave their car at the hotel.

This will leave from outside the Crown Plaza Hotel on the afternoon of the wedding at approximately 2.30pm to ensure guests arrive in time for the 3pm ceremony start. The bus will then be on hand from around midnight to make trips back over to the hotel.

We, Barry & Gillian, also plan to stay in the The Crown Plaza the night after our wedding - Friday 30th Sept - if you fancy continuing the high jinx, you're very welcome to join us.

If you are planning on staying in The Crown Plaza - you'll need to phone on +353 (0) 42 939 4900 and mention "McGee Wedding" to avail of the special rate offered to our wedding guests.
